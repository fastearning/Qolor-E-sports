Fair Play Policy

At Qolor E-Sports, we are committed to providing a fair, competitive, and enjoyable experience for all players. To ensure this, we have established the following Fair Play Policy that all participants are required to follow.

Our Commitment

We strive to maintain a gaming environment that values:

  • Integrity and honesty in competition.
  • Respect for all participants, regardless of skill level.
  • Equality of opportunity and access to fair gameplay.

Fair Play Guidelines

By participating in Qolor E-Sports events, you agree to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • No cheating, hacking, or use of third-party tools that provide unfair advantages.
  • Respect the decisions of tournament organizers and referees.
  • Avoid toxic behavior, including harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.
  • Play within the rules set for each tournament or event.
  • Do not engage in collusion or match-fixing of any kind.

Consequences of Violations

Violations of this policy will result in appropriate action, which may include:

  • Temporary or permanent bans from tournaments.
  • Disqualification from ongoing events.
  • Loss of winnings or rewards.

Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience any violations of our Fair Play Policy, please report them to our support team at qoloresportshelp@gmail.com.

Together, we can create a competitive and fun gaming community. Thank you for being a part of Qolor E-Sports!
